# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Meow Printer is an Android app for connecting to compatible BLE thermal printers and printing images or composed documents from your device. Meow Printer does not create user accounts, does not show ads, does not use analytics, and does not send your data to the developer's servers.

## Data processed by Meow Printer

Meow Printer may access, process, and store the following data on your device:

- Selected BLE printer name and address
- App preferences, such as print energy, dithering, and print pacing
- Images you choose to print or edit
- Document drafts and files you save or load with the compose feature
- Session logs generated for troubleshooting

This information is stored on your device unless you explicitly export or share it.

## How Meow Printer uses data

Meow Printer uses your data only to provide app features, including:

- Scanning for and connecting to a compatible BLE printer
- Printing images and composed documents to the selected printer
- Storing printer preferences and the currently selected printer
- Saving and loading composed document files when you request it
- Cropping or preparing images for printing on your device
- Showing connection, scan, and print logs for troubleshooting

## Data sharing

Meow Printer does not automatically share your data with the developer or third parties.

Data may leave your device only when you choose to do one of the following:

- Save or export a document file
- Share or back up files outside the app
- Upload files you created with the app to another service

## Permissions

Meow Printer may request Android permissions such as:

- Bluetooth permissions, to scan for and connect to compatible BLE printers
- Notification permission, to show active print notifications and allow background print cancellation on supported Android versions

These permissions are used only for app functionality on your device.

## Data retention

Meow Printer stores app data on your device until you remove it, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.

- Printer settings and preferences remain until you change them, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.
- Compose drafts remain until you replace them, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.
- Saved or exported files remain wherever you stored them until you delete them there.
- Session logs remain until you clear them, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.

Meow Printer does not have a cloud backend, so it does not keep server-side copies of your data.

## How to delete your data

You can delete Meow Printer data in the following ways:

- Clear the app's storage in Android settings
- Uninstall the app
- Delete any saved or exported files from the location where you stored them
- Clear the in-app session log

## Children's privacy

Meow Printer is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future app releases. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy text and date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the project repository or the app distribution page where you obtained Meow Printer.
